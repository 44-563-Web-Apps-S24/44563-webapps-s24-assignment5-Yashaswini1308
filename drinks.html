While reducing the size of the window slowly,we will observe that the row featuring the names of drinks breaks sooner than the row containing letters. This is because of that longer drink names needs more horizontal space. As the window width diminishes, these names exceed their allotted column space, forcing them to wrap onto the subsequent line. In contrast, single-character letters effortlessly stack vertically.Therefore, the primary reason for the breakage of drink names is their length and the constrained horizontal space.
